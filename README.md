# TheWorldPlays
Discover new music every day! 

Here's The World Plays — your daily underground music discovery site! 🌍
Here's how it works:
The Daily Algorithm
Uses today's date as a mathematical seed → picks the same artist for everyone visiting that day, no matter when or where they open it
Rotates through 50 hand-curated underground artists — Shovkat Mirzayev from Uzbekistan, Huun-Huur-Tu from Tuva, Noura Mint Seymali from Mauritania, Tanya Tagaq (Inuit throat singing from Canada), and many more
Absolutely zero mainstream acts — these are artists most people have never heard of.
